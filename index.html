Digital Access & Broadband in Oakland

Reliable internet access supports education, work, healthcare, and civic participation. The City is coordinating long-term efforts to expand affordable, reliable broadband access.

Read the Broadband Master Plan (PDF)

Guiding Strategy

The City’s long-term work is guided by the City of Oakland Broadband Master Plan (2025). This plan outlines goals for infrastructure, affordability, and digital equity.

Timeless, low-update approach

This page stays current by anchoring to durable resources (like the Master Plan) and stable service pathways.

How this work shows up

  • Planning and coordination across City departments
  • Broadband infrastructure investments and construction transparency
  • Partnerships with housing, schools, libraries, and community organizations
  • Resident pathways to free and low-cost internet options

Oakland Connect: Citywide Broadband Infrastructure

Oakland Connect is the City’s long-term effort to expand publicly owned broadband infrastructure and improve access citywide. It includes last-mile investments, affordable housing connectivity, and coordination with regional networks.
